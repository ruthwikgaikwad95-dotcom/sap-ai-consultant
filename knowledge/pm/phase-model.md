# Phase-Based Maintenance (PBM)

Module: SAP PM

Availability:

* SAP S/4HANA Asset Management
* Not available in classic ECC PM

Business Function:

* LOG_EAM_PBOM

Purpose:

* Manage maintenance work using phases
* Improve planning and execution control
* Support shutdown and turnaround projects

Key Concepts:

* Maintenance Item
* Maintenance Plan
* Maintenance Phase
* Phase Control

Important Notes:

* Do not confuse with classic maintenance strategy plans.
* PBM is part of S/4HANA Asset Management innovations.
* Often used together with Maintenance Planner and Fiori applications.

Relevant Fiori Apps:

* Manage Maintenance Plans
* Manage Maintenance Items

Testing Scenarios:

1. Create maintenance item
2. Create maintenance plan
3. Assign maintenance phases
4. Generate maintenance call
5. Verify phase progression

Common Mistakes:

* Confusing PBM with strategy-based maintenance.
* Assuming PBM exists in ECC.
* Missing required business function activation.
